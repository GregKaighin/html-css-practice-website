About me

Hello, my name is Greg Kaighin, thank you for visiting my website. I have a lifelong passion for music and have been playing the piano for over 30 years and working as a professional musician and piano teacher for more than 20 of those years. I am happy to teach people of all ages and abilities from beginner to advanced. Each of my students follows a dynamic, tailor-made learning plan which is designed to enable effective and enjoyable musical progress.

My goal is to equip my students with the skills, knowledge and mindset necessary to become independent musicians capable of following their own interest in music. This is achieved by developing a broad range of skills in performance, sight-reading, aural-awareness, composition and improvisation. I use modern teaching methods and books in addition to my own materials. I teach classical, popular and jazz piano music. Preparation for grade exams is available. I am also able to teach lessons in music theory and music technology.

I am DBS-checked and my certificate is available to view on request. Lessons are available either online via Zoom, or in your own home if you live in the Bedfordshire area. For beginner to intermediate level students, one 30-minute lesson each week is appropriate. For advanced students 60-minute lessons are recommended.

Tuition is available throughout the academic year including during school holidays if requested. Additional group workshops are held during school holidays in collaboration with other local teachers of different instruments, giving students an opportunity to participate in group music-making.

If you are interested in piano lessons, or music theory/music technology lessons, then please get in contact using the enquiry form or send an email to gregkaighin@hotmail.com, and I will get back to you soon.

Why Learn Piano with Me?

Experienced Teacher

I have more than twenty years of experience as a teacher. I studied at Leeds College of Music and gained a BA (Hons) degree in Jazz Studies.

Professional Musician

I have performed professionally for many years, both as a bandleader and also accompanying well-known acts in shows around the UK.

Tailored Learning

Each of my students follows a well-structured learning plan, suited to their individual requirements.

All Ages & Abilities Welcome
