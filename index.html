About me

Hello my name is Greg Kaighin, I am an experienced piano teacher who has been working for more than twenty years. I am happy to teach people of all ages and abilities from beginner to advanced level. Each of my pupils follows a dynamic tailor-made learning plan designed for making effective and enjoyable musical progress.

My aim is to equip pupils with the skills, knowledge and mindset necessary to become independent musicians capable of following their own interest in music. This is achieved by developing a range of skills in performance, sight-reading, aural-awareness, composition, improvisation and music theory. I use modern teaching methods and books in addition to my own materials. I teach classical, popular and jazz piano music. Preparation for grade exams is available.

I am DBS-checked and my certificate is available to view on request. Lessons are available either online via Zoom, or in your own home if you live in the Bedfordshire area. For beginner to intermediate level pupils, one 30-minute lesson each week is appropriate. For advanced pupils 60-minute lessons are recommended.

I am also a professional musician and run my own band.

If you are interested in piano lessons for yourself or your child, please get in contact using the enquiry form or send an email to gregkaighin@hotmail.com, and I will get back to you soon.
