Why Learn Piano with Me?

Experienced Tutor

I have over 20 years of experience as a tutor. I have a BA (Hons) degree in Jazz Studies from the Leeds College of Music.

Professional Musician

I have performed professionally for many years, both as a bandleader with my own group Bellissimo, and also accompanying well-known acts in shows around the UK.

Tailored Learning

All of my pupils follow a well-structured learning plan, tailored to their individual requirements.

All Ages & Abilities Welcome

I firmly believe that piano lessons and music-making should be enjoyable and accessible for everyone.
